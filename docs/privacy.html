Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-14 · App: Kibo — Custom Keyboard for iPhone (com.douchaojun.kibo)

Kibo (“the App”, “we”) is a custom keyboard for iPhone. We built Kibo to respect your privacy: we do not collect any data, we have no servers, no accounts, and no analytics. This policy explains exactly what the App does and does not do with your information. By using the App you agree to this policy.

1. Full Access is optional and off by default

Kibo’s keyboard extension declares RequestsOpenAccess = true, so iOS shows an “Allow Full Access” switch for Kibo. This switch is turned off by default. You only enable it if you choose to, in Settings → General → Keyboard → Keyboards → Kibo.

When Full Access is OFF (the default), iOS prevents the keyboard from accessing the network or the system clipboard. Only the copy/paste feature is unavailable; everything else — typing, themes, sound effects, light effects, emoji, moving the cursor, and deleting words — works normally.

When Full Access is ON, iOS allows the keyboard to read and write the system clipboard. Kibo uses this capability only for the copy-word, copy-line, and paste buttons in its toolbar. Even with Full Access enabled, Kibo still does not collect, log, or transmit anything you type, and Kibo has no servers to send anything to.

2. We do not collect your keystrokes

  • We do not collect, log, store, or transmit anything you type — no characters, words, sentences, passwords, or messages. This is true whether Full Access is on or off.
  • Everything you type is passed directly to the app you are typing into, exactly as iOS intends. Nothing is retained by Kibo.
  • We do not build any profile, typing history, or dictionary from your input.
  • If you enable Full Access and use the copy/paste buttons, clipboard contents are handled only on your device to perform that action. Kibo does not store, log, or transmit your clipboard.

3. The only data Kibo stores

The App stores a small amount of preference data so the keyboard behaves the way you configured it. This is the complete list:

  • The theme ID you selected.
  • Your sound effect, light effect, and haptic feedback on/off settings.
  • Your auto-capitalization and number row on/off settings.
  • Your recently used emoji, so the emoji panel can show them first.

All of this is stored locally on your device inside a shared App Group container belonging to Kibo. It never leaves your device. It is not uploaded, not backed up to our servers (we have no servers for it), and not shared with anyone.

4. No analytics, no advertising, no tracking

  • Kibo contains no third-party analytics SDKs.
  • Kibo contains no advertising SDKs and shows no ads.
  • We do not track you across apps or websites, and we do not sell or share data with data brokers.
  • We do not collect device identifiers, location, contacts, or any personally identifiable information.

5. Accounts

Kibo has no user accounts. There is no sign-up, no sign-in, no email address, and no profile.

6. No purchases

Kibo is free and contains no in-app purchases and no subscriptions. We do not process, receive, or store any payment information.

7. Account Deletion

Kibo has no accounts and collects no personal data, so there is no account to delete and no server-side data to erase.

The only data that exists is the small set of local preferences listed in Section 3, stored on your own device. Deleting the Kibo app from your iPhone permanently deletes all of that data. Nothing remains with us, because nothing was ever sent to us.

If you have any question about deletion, contact support@douhouse.com.

8. Children’s privacy

Kibo does not knowingly collect any information from children, and in fact collects no personal information from any user regardless of age.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If the App’s behavior ever changes — for example, if a future feature would collect or transmit data — we will update this page before that change ships, and the “Last updated” date above will reflect it.

10. Contact us

For any privacy question, contact: support@douhouse.com
